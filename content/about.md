<h1 class="about-header">Who am I?</h1>
<a href="/wp-content/uploads/2006/06/p8xGGmGe_400x400.jpg"><img class="alignright wp-image-2130 size-thumbnail" src="/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/profile8-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150" /></a>Passionate Linux user since 1998 working as a Software Engineer since 2006. Mainly interested in Open Source Software and the development of Web browsers and Linux-based Operating Systems.

Co-owner of <a href="https://www.igalia.com">Igalia</a>, where I currently work c<span aria-hidden="true">oordinating and managing the <a href="https://webkit.org/">WebKit</a> team, which includes the development of maintenance of the <a href="https://webkitgtk.org/">WebKitGTK</a> and the <a href="https://wpewebkit.org/">WPE WebKit</a> <em>ports</em>. Besides that, I also work on business development and project management.</span>

Additionally, I'm a member of the <a title="The GNOME Foundation" href="http://www.gnome.org/foundation/">GNOME Foundation</a> and the lead developer of a simple -yet powerful- <a title="Flickr" href="http://www.flickr.com/">flickr</a> uploader for the <a title="The GNOME Foundation" href="http://www.gnome.org">GNOME desktop</a> called <a title="Frogr: a Flickr Remote Organizer" href="https://wiki.gnome.org/Apps/Frogr">frogr</a>, which I work on in my spare time.

You can find more details in the <em><a title="Projects I worked/work on" href="/work">Work</a></em> and <a title="Talks and lectures" href="/talks/"><em>Talks</em></a> sections in this website, as well as in <a href="http://linkedin.com/in/mariosanchezprada">my profile at LinkedIn.</a>

Besides work related stuff, I love spending my time with family and friends, travelling, listening to music and taking pictures.
