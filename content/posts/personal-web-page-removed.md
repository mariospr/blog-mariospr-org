Just in case you wonder why my personal webpage (formerly at <a href="http://people.igalia.com/msanchez">http://people.igalia.com/msanchez</a>) is no longer available, here you are some brief explanations:
<ul>
	<li>I don't see the point of having a so simple webpage when the same thing could be easily got by setting up a <em><a title="Wordpress.org" href="http://wordpress.org" target="_blank">Wordpress</a> page</em>.</li>
	<li>I don't like having two different places to make all the stuff I want available for everybody: a <em>blog</em> for posts, and the personal web for the remaining stuff. Two different places also mean, by the way, two different "things" to maintain and keep up-to-date.</li>
	<li>I'm not a graphical designer and, no matter what I do, a webpage made and designed by me (I'm talking about the <em>CSS</em> on here, mainly) never will look as good as many <em><a title="Wordpress.org" href="http://wordpress.org" target="_blank">Wordpress</a></em> themes out there, like this one I'm currently using, for instance.</li>
</ul>
So, I just decided today to remove my personal webpage from the internet and set up a redirection to this blog (I don't like broken links either). But first, instead of just throwing away all the stuff I had in my old web, I've merged and moved them into the (already existing) <a title="About me" href="/about/"><em>About me</em></a> and the (newly created) <a title="Stuff" href="/stuff/"><em>Stuff</em></a> pages in this blog, respectively, whose hyperlinks you can find in the sidebar.

I hope this change made sense for you too :-)
