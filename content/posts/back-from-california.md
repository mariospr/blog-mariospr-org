As my mate <a title="Berto's blog" href="https://blogs.igalia.com/berto">Berto</a> has already <a title="Back from Mobile Rules post" href="https://blogs.igalia.com/berto/2008/03/26/back-from-mobile-rules/">commented on his blog</a>, we're back from California after attending to the <a title="Mobile Rules!" href="http://www.mobilerules.org" target="_blank">Mobile Rules!</a> '08 awards ceremony, and I'd like to say that we had a great time there, even though we didn't win the prize... but being a finalist was already a very nice kind of price, don't you think?

<a href="/wp-content/uploads/2008/03/20080318-MarioSanJose.jpg"><img src="/wp-content/uploads/2008/03/20080318-MarioSanJose_thumb.jpg" alt="Mario at the San Jose City Hall Rotunda" align="middle" /></a>

Moreover, maybe we didn't get the <span style="cursor: pointer">acknowledgement of being the </span><em> "Mobile Ruler '08"</em> under the <em>Multimedia</em> category, but we met a lot of people indeed, and we enjoyed a lot of the event, which was nicely arranged to allow all the participants to meet each other and to have a good dinner at the <a title="San Jose City Hall" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Jose_City_Hall" target="_blank">San Jose City Hall Rotunda</a> (look at the picture to see me in front of the beautiful San Jose City Hall).

So, maybe the next time we could have more luck with <a title="Vagalume's web site" href="http://vagalume.igalia.com">Vagalume</a>, but now it's the present and, as <a title="Berto's blog" href="https://blogs.igalia.com/berto">Berto</a> said in his last post, it's time to get back to work and keep on getting Vagalume even better with some nice improvements we already have in mind and others that I'm sure are still to come.

PS: This was the first time I've been to the US West Coast, and I have to say that I loved California... <em>such a lovely place, such a lovely place</em>. I hope I could go back soon :-)
