<a title="GUADEC" href="http://www.guadec.org"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-278" src="/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/guadec2010-attending.png" alt="I am attending GUADEC" width="134" height="46" /></a>

I've arrived yesterday to Den Haag, along with most of the <a title="Planet Igalia" href="http://www.igalia.com">Igalia</a> gang (still some of us coming tomorrow), and will be here just until Friday  morning, since I'll need to miss the last day of <a title="GUADEC" href="http://www.guadec.org">GUADEC</a> to attend my brother's wedding.
